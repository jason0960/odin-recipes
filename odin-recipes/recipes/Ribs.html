Ribs

Description

Ribs are great for a cook out or family gathering. This guide will show you how to properly cook them.

ingredents

  • Pork Ribs, any style
  • Sauce
  • Seasoning
  1. Removing membrane from back of ribs
  2. Wrap ribs in foil and cook at 275 for 3 hours
  3. Slather on sauce
  4. Cook another 15-30 minutes or until sauce thickens and glazes.
