Lasagna

Description

Lasagana is a meal that many people enjoy but few know how to make. This will guide you to make one.

ingredents

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Meat
  1. Boil your pasta
  2. Spread your pasta and cover with sauce
  3. Layer meat upon sauce
  4. Continue steps 1-3 4 times
  5. Cook in oven at 350 for 45 Min
